Privacy Policy

SmartBudget is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly.

Information We Collect

  • We store only the data you enter, such as your transactions and budgeting information, locally on your device.
  • SmartBudget does not collect, transmit, or share your personal or financial data with any third parties.

Use of Data

Your data remains on your device and is never sent to our servers or shared externally. This ensures your financial information stays private.

Cookies and Tracking

We utilize minimal cookies to improve site performance and user experience. No third-party trackers are used on this platform.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data and may clear your browser data at any time to remove your information from SmartBudget.
