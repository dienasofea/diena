Help & Support Center

We're Here to Help

Find answers to common questions, learn best practices, and get in touch with our support team.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add income or expenses?
Navigate to the Budget Book page or use the quick-add cards on the home page. Enter the date, description, and amount, then click the corresponding button to record your transaction.
Can I edit or delete transactions?
Yes. Go to the Transactions page where you'll find Edit and Delete buttons for each entry. Click Edit to modify details or Delete to remove a transaction permanently.
Why isn't my balance updating?
Ensure you've entered the correct amount and selected the appropriate category. The system automatically calculates your balance based on Income and Expense categories.
How do I view my spending by category?
Visit the Reports & Insights page to see a visual breakdown of your transactions by category, along with financial insights and recommendations.
Is my financial data secure?
Your data is stored locally in your browser. We never transmit, store, or sell your financial information. You maintain complete control and privacy.
